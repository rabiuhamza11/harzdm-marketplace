# HarzDM Marketplace

The Harz digital marketplace — buy and sell digital products globally.

## Overview
Full-stack digital marketplace where sellers list digital products and buyers purchase via Stripe. 90/10 revenue split — 90% to sellers, 10% platform fee.

## Features
- Product listings with categories, pricing & descriptions
- Stripe checkout — secure, global payments
- Instant digital download after purchase
- Seller dashboard — manage listings, track sales
- Admin panel — orders, revenue, seller management

## Revenue Model
90% to Seller | 10% to Platform
